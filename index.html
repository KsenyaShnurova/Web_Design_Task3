About

Who we are

We are Skyler and Finley. We were both born and raised in Colorado. Adventure sports are in our blood. We also understand that we live in a location full of unique, sensitive biodiversity. By taking tourists on guided tours we help to reduce the footprint, boost local economy, and keep non-locals off delicate ecosystems and restricted land. We can provide a service for tourism while still preserving the delicate ecosystem we live in.

The company

Based in Durango, Colorado, USA, we own and operate Explorers Southwest - a three-season (March to October) "mild to mildly adventurous" tours company. Our flagship and most popular offer - which started our business off in 2008 - was guided jeep tours to local "ghost mining towns" in the mountains between the towns of Durango and Silverton. We also offer a fully guided tour to Mesa Verde National Park one hour away. We rent stand up paddle boards (SUP), single person tubes and kayaks to use at nearby Lake Nighthorse.

Jeep tours

Our guided jeep tours take place in the mountains between Durango and Silvertone. We will show you a numerous wildflowers and rare plants, a large variety wildlife, high-alpine meadows, avalanche gullies and impressive waterfalls and geological features.

Day trips

Spend a whole day exploring the Mesa Verde National Park - one of only 20 World Heritage Sites in North America. Enjoy breathtaking views of mountains ranges from 4 states (Colorado, Arizona, Utah and New Mexico) on the way to Far View Ancestral Cillage.
