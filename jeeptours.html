Guided jeep tours

Our guided jeep tours take place in the mountains between Durango and Silvertone. We will show you a numerous wildflowers and rare plants, a large variety wildlife, high-alpine meadows, avalanche gullies and impressive waterfalls and geological features. A great option for families and large groups.

Follow the La Plata River to La Plata City, an old mining town established in 1875. Continue to the peak of Kennebec Pass at 12,000 feet above sea level. See all the way to Cortez and Telluride from the top of the pass!

Kids are extra entertained with our "Pan For Gold" scavenger hunt. Find the items on the checklist and get some fool's gold!

The roads are not well maintained and not often used which results in a bumpy, bouncy tour! Bring sun cover, suncream, a reusable water bottle, snacks, and your camera!
