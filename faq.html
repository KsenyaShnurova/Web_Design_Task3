Frequently asked questions

If you have any doubts, try to find it on the list. Is it not here? Contact us!

Are your services family friendly?

Yes, most of our activities are family friendly. We provide children with many interesting activities during the trips. We understand how important it is to spend some time together!

Are your services available all year?

We operate on three-seasons basis: from March to October.

Are you trained in touristic guidance?

Yes, not only we are fully trained tourist guides - we have been living in Colorado our whole lives. We are very passionate and professional about our services.

How should I prepare for the trip?

Each activity has its own preparation advice in its description. It depends heavily on the weather but ideal would be to prepare for any possibility. Do not forget water and suncream!

Do you offer any services other than those on your website?

We are planning to set up "glamping" pods next season. The details will be announced soon, look out for them!
