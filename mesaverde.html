Day trips to Mesa Verde National Park

Spend a whole day exploring the Mesa Verde National Park - one of only 20 World Heritage Sites in North America. Enjoy breathtaking views of mountains ranges from 4 states (Colorado, Arizona, Utah and New Mexico) on the way to Far View Ancestral Cillage.

Meeting at the Explorers Southwest Main Office at 8am for 8:30am start. Relax in our fully air conditioned tour bus while we take a 45 minute drive to Mesa Verde National Park on old Spanish Trail Trade Routes. We will then go straight to the Mesa Verde visitor center just inside the park for an introductory park orientation.

We will explore Cliff Palace, the largest and most famous cliff dwelling in North America, take in a variety of archeological sites, cliff dwellings, pit house and scenic overlooks.

Kids can earn their "Junior Park Ranger Badge" while interacting with one of Mesa Verse's park rangers.

Full day includes a robust packed lunch from the local deli (vegetarian and vegan options available upon request). Return by 5pm. Bring sturdy walking shoes, sun cover, suncream, a reusable water bottle and your camera!
